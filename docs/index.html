Load Windows .cur and .ani cursor packs and apply them straight to your Mac system cursors.

Download — Free macOS 15 Sequoia or later · Apple Silicon & Intel

I wanted to use a Windows cursor pack on my Mac. Turns out there wasn't a clean way to do that.

So I made Cursie. Drop in a cursor folder, see every role mapped out, and hit apply. That's the whole thing.

— kwd421
  • Live preview Every role mapped and visible before you apply. Actual size and zoomed side by side.
  • One-click apply Replaces your system cursors instantly. One click to restore defaults if you change your mind.
  • Drag & drop Drop a folder or file straight onto the app. Swap individual roles without starting over.
  • Auto-update Updates download and install inside the app. No manual trips to GitHub.

Cursie wouldn't exist without Mousecape by Alex Zielenski — the open-source cursor manager that pioneered .cape themes on macOS. Standing on the shoulders of that work.
